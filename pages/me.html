about me

Hello, thank you for visiting my website! My name is Christine Minjae Kwon, and I am a 21 year-old digital creative. I love making stuff, ranging from web and mobile applications to graphic illustrations, 3d models, music, and more!


I am a first-generation Korean-American who grew up experiencing a plethora of cities including San Diego, Austin, San Jose, and Seoul.


I recently finished up my junior year at Princeton University, where I am pursuing a degree in computer science, focusing on pondering the ways in which technology interacts with society, and experimenting with ways I can bring joy to this world we live in.


I am currently residing in Seoul, Korea, working on independent projects and exploring my creative side, while conducting preliminary research for my senior thesis.
