garlic
A new language of time displays the current time by the degrees of rotation of garlic cloves.
watch interface animation tool
random animation player
This project began as a simple idea of allowing the garlic to decay over time and associating the state of decay with the current time of day.. but playing around in three.js with the 3 dimensional garlic clove shapes that I created in Maya led to unexpected visual effects that led me in a different direction. The garlic clock displays the time of day based on how much the cloves rotate. The cloves make a full 360 degree rotation over the course of a day beginning at midnight, so one can tell the time of day based on the shape of the bulb at any given moment.
